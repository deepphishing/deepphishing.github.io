The scenarios are realistic and can be tailored to your company. You‘re not limited to just a few emails either, the service scales to thousands of emails with A/B campaigns to see what kind of threat your employees are most vulnerable to in a non-threatening environment

Regular campaigns will give you the information you need to empower your Security and HR departments; give assurance to your board that you’re taking appropriate action, and demonstrate that you’re making efficient use of your budget.

With the Deep Phishing service it will be possible to see exactly how susceptible you are to phishing attacks and how far an attacker could gain access if one of your employees were to fall victim of a real attack.

Valuable data on what vulnerabilities could have been exploited for each employees tricked by the campaign is shown in easy to understand graphic format. A detailed technical list of which machines were vulnerable is included in the report. With this information, you will have a clear and full picture of how at risk your desktops are. If you have a high instance of Java vulnerabilities, for example, you might find that the underlying reason is because you have many different versions of JVM’s. Knowing this allows you to react and remediate appropriately.
